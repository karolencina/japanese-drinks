Kombucha

You got Kombucha! Kombucha is a slightly acidic, fermented drink made from a base of tea, sugar, bacteria and yeast and is as old as 2,000 years. To make kombucha, a symbiotic colony of bacteria and yeast (SCOBY) is added to the tea and sugar, and the mixture is allowed to ferment. Although it's tea-based, Kombucha tastes nothing like tea but more like a mixture of cider (it's fizzy), wine and beer.
