Umeshu

You got Umeshu! Plum Wine or Umeshu (梅酒) is a Japanese liqueur made by steeping fresh Japanese plum (ume) in shochu (white liquor) and sugar. Its has sweet and sour flavors with fruity aroma and can be either drank on its own or used as a base of cocktails like Ume Highball (a ginger drink), Umeko or "plum child" (a lemon drink).
