Sakurayu

You got Sakurayu! Cherry-blossom tea or sakurayu (桜湯) is a hot drink with a salt-preserved cherry blossom (sakurazuke) in it. The flowers are bottled in such a manner that the flower closes up in a ball along with the stem. When hot water is poured over the cherry blossom, the salt melts, whereupon the petals open up and float on the surface of the water. The tea is served instead of green tea during once-in-a-lifetime ceremonies like marriage.
